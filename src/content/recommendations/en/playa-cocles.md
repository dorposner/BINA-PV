The most vibrant and popular beach in the area, attracting surfers from all over the world thanks to its great waves. While the currents make it less suitable for relaxed swimming, it's the perfect place to lay a towel on the soft sand, watch the surfers in action, and soak up the young, carefree Caribbean atmosphere.
