The classic, greenest, and most fun way to get around the area! You can rent a bicycle (available on every corner) and casually pedal along the flat road connecting Puerto Viejo to the southern beaches. The route is gorgeous, allowing spontaneous stops at any beach or restaurant that catches your eye.
