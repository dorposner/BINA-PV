For those looking for a bit of movement and a breathtaking view. This is a relatively short but fairly steep hiking trail that leads you to a high point in the area. At the end of the climb, a very special reward awaits: a stunning panoramic view over the entire Cocles coastline and the surrounding jungle. Make sure to bring water and head out in the early morning hours.
