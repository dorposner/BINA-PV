A beautiful national park located about 20 minutes from Puerto Viejo. The park offers easy walking trails along the beach, excellent wildlife viewing (monkeys, sloths, snakes, birds, and more), and the option to snorkel in the coral reef.
Entrance to the park (from the town of Cahuita) is based on a voluntary donation. It is recommended to arrive early in the morning!
