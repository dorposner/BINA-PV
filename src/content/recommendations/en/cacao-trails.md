A fascinating journey into the heart of local tradition. The farm offers an educational and fun experience with in-depth cacao tours — from the tree all the way to the production of natural chocolate. From the very entrance, you can spot monkeys running through the trees and see an enormous variety of banana species and tropical plants. Highly recommended for families and nature lovers who want to taste real, high-quality chocolate straight from the source.
