One of the most beautiful, calm, and photogenic beaches in the Puerto Viejo area. The quiet waters make it a classic beach for safe swimming and relaxing. For those looking for a bit more action, you can rent a kayak and paddle along the coast or up the nearby river to see the jungle and wildlife from a slightly different angle.
