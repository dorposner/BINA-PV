A uniquely different experience at the northern edge of Puerto Viejo. This is a beach with stunning black sand belonging to the hotel, but open to the general public. The vibe is more luxurious and relaxed; you can order fresh smoothies from the bar, use their comfortable beach chairs, and even enjoy the hotel pool. An ideal spot for those seeking a little pampering and comfort in front of the powerful black sea.
