At the end of the southern road, where the asphalt ends, hides this reserve offering wild nature in all its glory. You will find fascinating hiking trails through the jungle that spill onto secluded beaches. On days when the sea is calm, it's an excellent spot for snorkeling and encountering the underwater world.
