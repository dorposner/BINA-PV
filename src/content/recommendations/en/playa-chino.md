The most central and accessible beach within Puerto Viejo. A shallow and pleasant stretch of sand, ideal for a relaxed swim or simply dipping your feet while strolling through the village. What makes Playa Chino special is the lively atmosphere surrounding it — the beach is lined with restaurants, cafes, and pubs that sit right on the water's edge. On certain evenings you can enjoy live music on the sand, adding to the Caribbean rhythm of the place.
