One of the most iconic and photogenic spots in Puerto Viejo. A sunken boat (El Barco Hundido) sits in the shallow waters and has become the landmark of this stretch of beach. The water is very comfortable for swimming, and the beach itself is wide and shaded in the upper section — making it a hugely popular spot with tourists and locals alike. This is the perfect place to meet new people, sit on the sand, and take in the unique scenery.
